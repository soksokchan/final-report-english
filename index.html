    Airport to school

    Taoyuan

  • High Speed Rail ：Take the high-speed rail shuttle bus to the high-speed rail Taoyuan Station → Take the high-speed rail from the high-speed rail Taoyuan Station to Chiayi Station → After arriving, take the high-speed rail ticket and take the BRT to Chiayi Railway Station for free.
  • Railways Administration ：Taoyuan Airport → Take the Taoyuan Passenger Transport to Taoyuan Railway Station → Take the Taitung Railway from Taoyuan Station to Chiayi Railway Station
  • Passenger transport ：Taoyuan Airport → Take the Tonglian and Guoguang passengers to Taichung Chaoma Station → Transfer from Taichung Chaoma Station to Chiayi Transit Station

    • Kaohsiung

  • High Speed Rail ：Take the MRT from Xiaogang Airport to Xinzuoying Station → Take the high-speed train to Chiayi High-speed Railway Station → High-speed rail connecting bus BRT to Chiayi Railway Station
  • Railways Administration ：Take the MRT Airport MRT to Kaohsiung Railway Station → Take the Taiwan Railway to Chiayi Railway Station
  • Passenger transport ：Xiaogang Airport take the MRT or taxi to Kaohsiung Zuoying Station → Take the Hexin Express to Chiayi Transit Station
